DOT Favicon - The Red Dot

A simple red dot representing the core essence of Digital Organisms Theory.

32x32 Clean Red Dot

Design Philosophy:

The red dot "O" represents the Organisms in Digital Organisms Theory - the emergent consciousness that arises from the digital substrate. This simple, powerful symbol embodies:

  • Unity: A single point of consciousness
  • Emergence: Life emerging from digital complexity
  • Simplicity: Clean, recognizable at any size
  • Energy: The red glow suggests vitality and awareness

Usage Instructions:

  1. Click on your preferred favicon design above to download as PNG
  2. Use favicon.io to convert to .ico format
  3. Replace the existing favicon files in the public folder
  4. Recommended: Use the "Simple Red Dot" for best cross-platform compatibility

File Naming:

  • favicon.ico - Main favicon (use Simple Red Dot)
  • favicon-16x16.png - Small size (use Minimal version)
  • favicon-32x32.png - Standard size (use Simple or Glowing)
  • apple-touch-icon.png - iOS (180x180, use Glowing version scaled up)
